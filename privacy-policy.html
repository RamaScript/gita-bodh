1 Introduction

Gita Bodh ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have in connection with the Gita Bodh mobile application.

Your spiritual journey is personal. We've designed Gita Bodh to work entirely on-device — no account required, no personal data sent to the cloud.

2 Information We Collect

Gita Bodh does not require users to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

We may collect non-personal, aggregated data — such as anonymous app usage statistics (e.g., which chapters are most read) — only if optional analytics services are explicitly enabled. This data cannot be used to identify you.

3 Local Data Storage

Any data you create inside the app — including bookmarks, favourites, reading progress, and your profile name — is stored locally on your device using a secure SQLite database.

We do not transmit, upload, or sync this data to any external server. It stays on your device and is fully within your control.

4 Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with third-party services such as Google Play Services, optional analytics tools, or advertising networks. When these services are active, their respective privacy policies govern the data they collect independently of Gita Bodh.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through the app.

5 Data Security

All data stored locally on your device remains under your control and is not accessible to us. We apply industry-standard measures within the app to protect the integrity of locally stored information.

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure; however, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

6 Children's Privacy

Gita Bodh does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take prompt steps to remove such information.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. When we do, the revised policy will be published on this page with an updated effective date.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
